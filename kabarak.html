KABARAK UNIVERSITY

ABOUT

History

Kabarak University was established in 2002 by the late Hon. Daniel T. arap Moi, the 2nd President of the Republic of Kenya. The university is located in Kabarak, Nakuru County, Kenya. It is a private Christian university that aims to provide quality education and promote moral and ethical values among its students.

Location

Kabarak University is situated in the scenic town of Kabarak, approximately 20 kilometers from Nakuru town. The university campus is nestled in a serene environment, providing an ideal setting for learning and personal growth.

ACADEMIC PROGRAMS

Academic programs in Kabarak University are spread across the following Seven Schools:

  1. Medicine and Health Sciences
  2. Education Humanities and Social Sciences
  3. Science Engineering and Technology
  4. Law
  5. Business and Economics
  6. Phamacy
  7. Music and Media
